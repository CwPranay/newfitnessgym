# **App Name**: New Age Fit

## Core Features:

- Navigable Website Structure: Comprehensive display of all static content sections, including Hero, About, Membership Plans, Trainers, Reviews, Gallery, and Contact, integrated with a sticky navigation bar for smooth user experience.
- Mobile-First Responsive Design: Ensures the website layout and content adapt seamlessly across various screen sizes, from mobile phones to desktop monitors, prioritizing a mobile user experience.
- WhatsApp Communication Gateway: Direct integration of clickable 'Join Now' and contact buttons that open WhatsApp chats with the gym's official phone number (8425919590), allowing for easy enrollment and inquiries.
- Interactive Location Map: An embedded Google Map displaying the gym's precise location (Shop No. 02, Kulswamini Paradise, Kalyan East, Mumbai, Maharashtra 421306) within the Contact section for easy navigation.
- Membership Plan Presentation: Clearly presents three distinct membership plans (Basic, Standard, Premium) with placeholder pricing, guiding users to select and inquire about their preferred fitness package.
- AI Fitness Motivation Tool: A tool that generates short, punchy motivational quotes or general fitness tips to keep users engaged and inspired on their fitness journey.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary accent color: A vibrant, electric blue (#2EC2FF) to signify energy, modernity, and a tech-forward approach in fitness, standing out powerfully against the dark backdrop.
- Background color: A deep, cool-toned dark grey (#131A1F), providing a premium, sleek, and high-contrast base for the design that evokes a sophisticated gym environment.
- Accent color: A bold, energetic orange (#FF9600) used for crucial call-to-action buttons and highlighted elements, ensuring high visibility and driving conversions.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), offering a modern, clean, and bold aesthetic that complements the fitness brand's contemporary image. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional legibility and neutral, objective appearance, ideal for conveying information clearly.
- Utilize clean, sharp, outline-style icons to maintain the modern and minimal aesthetic. Icons should visually reinforce section content without cluttering the premium interface.
- Employ a mobile-first responsive grid layout, prioritizing content legibility and navigation ease on smaller screens while gracefully scaling up for desktop viewing. Implement a sticky navigation bar and smooth scroll for fluid site exploration.
- Incorporate subtle scroll-triggered animations to enhance user engagement and provide a dynamic, modern feel without distracting from the core content or impacting performance.
